# /content/blog

MDX blog posts with gray-matter frontmatter. One post per file; slug matches filename.

## Frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: "Specific outcome or unusual noun: what this teaches"
slug: kebab-with-place-name
date: 2026-05-12
author: <founder-name>
tags: [primary-place, content-type, customer-type, specific-topic]
hero_image: /images/blog/<slug>-hero.jpg
excerpt: 25-30 word meta-description-quality summary
---
```

## The five blog patterns (use one per post)

1. **The "I'll Find Out" Story** — a weird customer request that reveals the customer-service standard
2. **The Recognition / Serendipity Story** — a chance encounter that turned into business; brand-recognition compound interest
3. **The Improbable Coincidence Story** — a statistical anomaly in your network; "known is better than best"
4. **The Give-First Story** — a small unselfish action with traceable downstream business value
5. **The Case-Study Story** — a specific dollar/MPH/college-acceptance outcome with a step-by-step process

## Cadence
1–2 posts per week (per the playbook). Drafts live in `/content/blog/_drafts/` and are excluded from the build until promoted.
